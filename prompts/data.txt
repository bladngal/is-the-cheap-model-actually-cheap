Clean it and analyze it:
1. Write cleaned_sales.csv with: duplicates removed, all dates normalized to YYYY-MM-DD, category names normalized to title case and trimmed, region normalized to title case with missing regions set to "Unknown", and rows missing units_sold OR unit_price dropped.
2. Write summary.md containing: total rows kept and dropped, total revenue (units_sold * unit_price), revenue by category (sorted descending), and revenue by month.

Use Python for the processing. When both files are written and correct, stop and briefly summarize the results.
